**The Issue:** Selling 495 Luxury Units in Ropar is a volume challenge.
*   **Current Narrative:** "Global Indian HNI."
*   **Missing Detail:** **Channel Strategy.** How specifically do we reach 495 NRIs?

## 4. The "Valuation" Gap (Real Estate vs. Tech) (OPEN)
**Severity: Medium**
**The Issue:** Real Estate investors want safe returns (18-24% IRR). Tech investors want 10x multiples.
*   **Conflict:** By self-funding Tech from RE profits, we solve the *funding* source but confuse the *exit*.
*   **The Question:** Does the RE investor get equity in the Tech Co (InjoyOS)? or just the PropCo?
*   **Recommendation:** Clarify the "Spin-out" structure. "Investors in PropCo get pro-rata rights in TechCo Series A," etc.
