Data Sovereignty

Indian Data Law Compliance:
Challenges & InjoyOS Solutions

InjoyOS processes continuous biometric data — ECG, SpO₂, HRV, gait, sleep, and behavioral patterns — making regulatory compliance not optional but foundational. Here's how we architect for India's evolving data landscape.

shield_lock

DPDP Act, 2023

Digital Personal Data Protection

warning The Challenge

Continuous biometric streams are classified as Sensitive Personal Data. Seniors with cognitive decline complicate consent requirements. AI-driven Digital Health Twins trigger automated decision-making restrictions. Data retention conflicts with longitudinal AI model needs.

check_circle InjoyOS Solution

  • All health data stored in Indian sovereign data centers — zero cross-border transfer
  • Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) architecture — AI flags risks but a licensed clinician must review and approve before any intervention, satisfying the Act's automated decision-making restrictions
  • Guardian-authorized consent framework for cognitively impaired residents
  • Purpose-limited data collection with granular consent management
  • Appointed Data Protection Officer (DPO) with right-to-erasure workflows
health_and_safety

ABDM Standards

Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission

warning The Challenge

ABDM requires FHIR R4-compliant health records, but continuous biometric streaming doesn't map cleanly to traditional EHR formats. Dual consent frameworks (ABDM + DPDP Act) must operate simultaneously. Every resident needs an ABHA health ID.

check_circle InjoyOS Solution

  • FHIR R4 data mapping engine translating continuous biometric streams to interoperable records
  • ABHA ID provisioning during resident onboarding
  • Registered as Health Information Provider (HIP) within the ABDM network
  • Unified consent layer satisfying both ABDM and DPDP Act requirements
video_call

MoHFW Telehealth Guidelines

Ministry of Health & Family Welfare

warning The Challenge

AI cannot prescribe — only licensed practitioners can. Clear boundaries needed between "AI suggestion" and "clinical decision." Cross-state medical licensing creates friction for remote consultations. Teleconsultation records require specific retention periods and audit trails.

check_circle InjoyOS Solution

  • AI recommendations clearly separated as Clinical Decision Support — never prescriptive
  • Licensed physician review required before any intervention
  • Automated escalation pathways from telehealth to in-person care
  • Complete teleconsultation audit trail with tamper-proof logging
security

CERT-In Directives

Cybersecurity Incident Response

warning The Challenge

Any cybersecurity incident must be reported within 6 hours — extremely tight for a 24/7 health monitoring system. IoT wearables are high-value attack vectors. Real-time biometric data in transit requires end-to-end encryption. System logs must be retained for 180 days minimum.

check_circle InjoyOS Solution

  • End-to-end AES-256 encryption for all biometric data streams
  • Automated 6-hour incident detection and CERT-In reporting pipeline
  • IoT device hardening with secure firmware OTA update management
  • 180-day log retention infrastructure with immutable audit logs
